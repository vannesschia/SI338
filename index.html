Bio

Garrett is a junior on the Skyline XC team. After a mediocre freshman year and a season-ending injury during sophomore year, Garrett was more determined than ever to run well. He became one of the most consistent runners on the team, earning a top-7 spot in every race. He stood out not only for his running, but also for motivating teammates by leading cheers, keeping energy high, and staying positive. His favorite memories come from team camp, where everyone spent the day playing games and still had energy left over. His favorite race was Holly, where the team placed first and went on stage together with a big trophy.
